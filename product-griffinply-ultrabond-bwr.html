Overview
GriffinPly Ultrabond BWR Grade Plywood is engineered for superior performance in moisture-prone environments. Manufactured using advanced bonding technology and high-grade hardwood veneers, it delivers exceptional strength, durability, and resistance to water.

Designed for both residential and commercial applications, Ultrabond is the perfect choice where reliability is non-negotiable.

Key Features

1. Boiling Water Resistant (BWR)

Withstands prolonged exposure to moisture and humidity — ideal for kitchens and semi-wet areas.

2. Ultrabond Technology

Enhanced adhesive formulation ensures stronger internal bonding and zero delamination.

3. High Structural Strength

Engineered core provides superior load-bearing capacity and long-term durability.

4. Termite & Borer Proof

Special chemical treatment protects against pests, ensuring longer life.

5. Warp & Crack Resistant

Maintains shape and stability even under changing weather conditions.
