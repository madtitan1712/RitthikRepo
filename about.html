Our Heritage

Crafting excellence since 1995.

Rooted in Quality

Based in the vibrant industrial hub of Dindigul, Tamil Nadu, GriffinPly has grown from a local enterprise into a trusted name across India. Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility harnesses the finest timber while adhering to rigorous international standards.

We combine decades of traditional woodworking expertise with modern engineering to produce plywood that stands the test of time. From sourcing the raw materials to the final quality checks, every sheet of GriffinPly reflects our unwavering commitment to strength, durability, and our South Indian roots.
