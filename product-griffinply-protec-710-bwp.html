Overview
GriffinPly Protec 710 BWP Grade Plywood is engineered for the most demanding environments where water exposure is constant and performance cannot fail. Built with premium hardwood veneers and bonded with high-quality phenolic resins, it offers exceptional waterproofing, strength, and longevity.

Designed for marine, industrial, and high-moisture applications, Protec 710 stands as the pinnacle of durability in the GriffinPly range.
