Overview
GriffinPly Gold MR Grade Plywood is crafted for interior applications where durability meets affordability. Designed with precision and manufactured using high-quality hardwood veneers, it offers excellent strength, smooth finish, and long-lasting performance.

Ideal for homes, offices, and commercial spaces, this plywood delivers consistent quality you can trust.

Key Features

1. Moisture Resistant (MR Grade)

Built to withstand humidity and minor moisture exposure, making it perfect for interior furniture.

2. Superior Bonding Strength

Manufactured using high-quality adhesives that ensure strong internal bonding and long life.

3. Smooth Surface Finish

Uniform surface for easy laminates, veneers, or paint application.

4. Termite & Borer Resistant

Chemically treated to protect against termites and wood-boring insects.

5. High Load Bearing Capacity

Strong core construction ensures durability for everyday use furniture.
